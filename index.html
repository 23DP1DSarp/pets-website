Facts About Pets!


Some facts about pets you didn't know about

Scroll down to know more

A dog’s sense of smell is legendary, but did you know that their nose has as many as 300 million receptors? In comparison, a human nose has about 5 million.

Some species are also known to use tools! There have been many studies on parrot behavior. One researched Greater Vasa Parrots, observing their use of date pits and pebbles as a tool to grind down cockle shells. The powder from the shells provides a hearty dose of calcium the parrots ingest!
